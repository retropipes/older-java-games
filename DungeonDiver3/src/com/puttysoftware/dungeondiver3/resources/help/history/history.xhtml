DungeonDiver3 Version History

 

V2.0.0 (05/04/2012):

New Features:

  • •Added the ability to rename Faiths to the Name Editor. 

  • •Added Faith Powers to the game: 

    • ◦Added a new shop, the Faith Power Shop, that sells these. 

    • ◦Faith Powers for weapons give a damage boost. 

    • ◦Faith Powers for armor give damage reduction. 

    • ◦Faith Powers, like Faiths, can be renamed in the Name Editor. 

  • •Added the ability to fire arrows in battle: 

    • ◦Arrows never miss. 

    • ◦Arrows are MUCH weaker than melee weapons. 

    • ◦Arrows obey faith rules when determining damage. 

  • •The battle engine now respects the “One Move at a Time” preference. 

Other Changes:

  • •Updated the saved game format version to 2. Older saved games still work. 

  • •Updated the names file version to 2. Old names files will be automatically deleted. 

  • •Made a tweak to the sound engine to hopefully further limit the random crashing. 

 

V1.3.1 (05/01/2012):

Bug Fixes:

  • •Dangerous ground now only does bad things if the battlefield randomization is turned on, as it was supposed to behave all along. 

 

V1.3.0 (04/30/2012):

New Features:

  • •Added the ability to edit the names of things in the game. Changes made take effect immediately. 

  • •Added an in-depth Statistics viewer. 

Other Changes:

  • •Raised the floor for hit rate from 50% to 80%, and lowered the ceiling for evade rate from 50% to 20%, making battles a lot less frustrating due to missing an awful lot. 

  • •Renamed the Show Item Inventory... menu command to Show Inventory... 

  • •Cleaned up the code a bit. 

 

V1.2.0 (04/25/2012):

New Features:

  • •Upgraded the drawing engine. No more weird and temporary breaks when the dungeon or battle maps get redrawn. 

  • •Added a new preference (defaults to on) to turn sound playback off/on. 

  • •Added a new preference (defaults to off) to randomize the battlefield. 

    • ◦When battlefield randomization is turned ON, walls and hostile ground are added in random locations to the battle map. 

    • ◦Hostile ground has its usual effects, hurting both your party members as well as the monsters, if they are foolish enough to step on it. 

    • ◦Walls block movement as normal. 

Other Changes:

  • •Renamed the Music pane in the Preferences to the Media pane. 

 

V1.1.0 (04/20/2012):

New Features:

  • •Added descriptions for races, castes, faiths, and personalities to the character creation interface. 

Bug Fixes:

  • •Removed a debugging print statement that was accidentally left in the 1.0.0 release. 

 

V1.0.0 (04/15/2012) (changes from V1.0.0-RC1):

New Features:

  • •Initial stable release. 

Bug Fixes:

  • •Fixed a minor platform integration issue on OS X. 

  • •Fixed a bug in the damage formula that made equipment too strong. 

  • •Fixed a double-count bug in attack and defense calculations.
